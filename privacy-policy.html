Privacy Policy Last updated: May 2026 📌 The Short Version: SenangDuit stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal information. Your financial data never leaves your phone. 1. Introduction Welcome to SenangDuit ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SenangDuit handles your information when you use our mobile application. SenangDuit is designed with privacy as a core principle. Unlike most apps, we have built SenangDuit to function entirely offline, meaning your sensitive financial data stays exclusively on your device. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device SenangDuit stores the following information only on your device: Expense records: Title, amount, date, category, notes you add Budget settings: Your monthly budget amount Loan calculations: Loan parameters you input (not saved beyond the calculation) App preferences: Theme choice (light/dark/system), onboarding status This data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We do NOT have access to it, and it is NOT transmitted to any server. 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect SenangDuit does NOT collect, transmit, or have access to: Your name, email, or any personal identification Your phone number or contact list Your location or GPS data Your device identifiers Your financial accounts or banking information Your browsing history or app usage data Any photos, files, or media on your device 3. How We Use Information Since all data is stored locally on your device, the app uses your information solely to: Display your expenses and budget information Calculate ML-powered insights, predictions, and analytics on-device Perform loan calculations Apply your visual preferences (theme, etc.) All data processing happens on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties because we do not collect or have access to your data in the first place. SenangDuit does not include any: Analytics tracking (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.) Advertising networks Crash reporting services Third-party SDKs that collect data 5. Data Security Your data is protected by: Device-level security: Your data is only accessible through your unlocked device App sandboxing: Android/iOS prevents other apps from accessing SenangDuit's data No cloud transmission: Data cannot be intercepted in transit because no transmission occurs For additional security, we recommend using a screen lock on your device. 6. Data Backup Your data may be included in your device's automatic backups (e.g., Google Drive backup on Android, iCloud on iOS) according to your device settings. These backups are managed by Google or Apple, not by us. Please review their privacy policies for details on how they handle backup data. 7. Children's Privacy SenangDuit is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed to be used by adults managing their own personal finances. 8. Permissions SenangDuit requests minimal permissions: Storage: To save your expense data locally on your device No internet permission is required for core functionality. 9. Your Rights and Control You have complete control over your data: View all your data: Available within the app at any time Edit any record: Tap any expense to modify it Delete individual records: Swipe to delete any expense Delete all data: Use "Clear All Data" in Settings Uninstall the app: Removes all data from your device permanently 10. Future Premium Features We may introduce optional premium features in the future. If any feature requires data transmission (e.g., cloud sync), we will: Update this privacy policy with detailed information Make data collection opt-in only (never enabled by default) Provide clear disclosure before any data leaves your device 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
