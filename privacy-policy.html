Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

📌 The Short Version: SenangDuit stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal information. Your financial data never leaves your phone.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SenangDuit ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SenangDuit handles your information when you use our mobile application.

SenangDuit is designed with privacy as a core principle. Unlike most apps, we have built SenangDuit to function entirely offline, meaning your sensitive financial data stays exclusively on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

SenangDuit stores the following information only on your device:

  • Expense records: Title, amount, date, category, notes you add
  • Budget settings: Your monthly budget amount
  • Loan calculations: Loan parameters you input (not saved beyond the calculation)
  • App preferences: Theme choice (light/dark/system), onboarding status

This data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We do NOT have access to it, and it is NOT transmitted to any server.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

SenangDuit does NOT collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identification
  • Your phone number or contact list
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers
  • Your financial accounts or banking information
  • Your browsing history or app usage data

3. How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the app uses your information solely to:

  • Display your expenses and budget information
  • Calculate ML-powered insights, predictions, and analytics on-device
  • Perform loan calculations
  • Apply your visual preferences (theme, etc.)

All data processing happens on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties because we do not collect or have access to your data in the first place.
